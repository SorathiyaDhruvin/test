About Parul University

A leading institution committed to academic excellence, innovation, and global learning.

:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} is a premier private university located in Vadodara, Gujarat. The university is known for its diverse academic ecosystem, industry-oriented programs, and strong focus on innovation, research, and practical learning.

With students from over 60+ countries, Parul University offers a truly global learning environment. The university provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across engineering, medical sciences, management, arts, pharmacy, law, and applied sciences.

The campus features modern laboratories, advanced research centers, digital classrooms, incubation hubs, hostels, sports complexes, and smart infrastructure designed to support holistic student development.

Our Mission

  • Provide global exposure and experiential learning
